Pricing page update, new services

2023.13.12 - Daniel

Finally got around to creating a pricing page to include all the services they offer. I created a quick access button on the homepage, but I feel like I should also create a button in the navbar so it's accessible from any page. Still need to create the contact and review pages, but I want to take my time to make them look good and match the vibe of the rest of the site. Currently need to:

  • Fix the gallery to show multiple rows of images without breaking the scrollbar
  • Create contact and review pages, and make them fully functional
  • Somehow make the site even more mobile friendly, maybe by making the container squeeze down to fit mobile screens?

November and December have been pretty good for us so far, and we are so grateful to everyone who gave us the chance to clean their cars. For anyone who hasn't, be sure to take advantage of our extended sale, it ends on 31 January. Also wanted to mention that we can do more than just detailing - if you need any basic work done to your car, we can take care of that too! If you need an oil change, we can do it for the cost of the oil and filter + $10. If you need a brake job, we can do it for the cost of the parts + $50 per axle. If you provide your own parts then that cost will be subtracted from the total, and if you are not sure what your car needs, we will always be very transparent with the price of anything we are purchasing for your car. I also just wanted to remind everyone that we also offer paint corrections, which always include an interior vacuum and full exterior wash and decontamination. Some exciting news - we are thinking of breaking into the ceramic coating market as well! Of course we will make sure to practice on our own vehicles until we are confidently proficient, but if anyone is thinking about getting a ceramic coating, we will be offering a significantly reduced rate for a limited time.
